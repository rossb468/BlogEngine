# Welcome to My Blog

Welcome to my corner of the internet. I started this blog as a place to document what I am learning, share techniques I find useful, and think out loud about software development. Writing about code forces me to understand it more deeply, and if something I write here saves someone else an afternoon of debugging, even better.

![A laptop on a clean desk with a cup of coffee](https://picsum.photos/seed/welcome-blog/800/400)

You can expect posts on a range of topics: Swift and iOS development will be front and center, but I also plan to explore software architecture patterns, developer tooling, and the occasional deep dive into something unexpected. I believe the best engineers are the curious ones -- the ones who ask *why* a system works the way it does, not just *how* to use it. That spirit of curiosity is what I hope to bring to every post.

Thanks for stopping by. If you have feedback, suggestions, or just want to say hello, feel free to reach out through [GitHub](https://github.com/rossb468) or via email. I am looking forward to building this space out over time.
